About Mwanamama Enterprises

Empowering Communities Since Day One

Who We Are

Mwanamama Enterprises Limited is a proudly Kenyan company headquartered in Hola, Tana River County. We empower families, groups, and businesses through affordable hire purchase, trusted Bajaj motorcycles, and professional event services.

From our community roots, we've grown into a multi-service enterprise guided by a simple belief: Empowerment begins with access.

Our Mission

To uplift families, groups, and communities by providing affordable access to essential goods, motorcycles, and event solutions through flexible, transparent, and locally grounded services.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted community enterprise, empowering lives through innovation, integrity, and inclusivity.

Core Values

  • Integrity - Honest dealings and accountability
  • Empowerment - Enabling ownership and independence
  • Trust - Consistent delivery and clear communication
  • Excellence - High standards in service and quality
  • Community - Shared success with a local-first mindset
